The technology Division at Mott Community College covers a variety of technology areas shaping the world today and in the future. The areas of robotics and information technology are important areas that cover programming, electronics, and automation that are being used both in academic and business environments.

To help students explore these critical technologies, the MCC Tech Division sponsors a Robotics Club and Tech Club working on various projects. These projects let students delve deeper and gain hands on experience.
